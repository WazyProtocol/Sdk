# Wazy

> Something changed him—undefined.  
> No memory. No beginning. No return.  
> Just the stillness of existence.

---

## 🧬 Overview

**Wazy** is not just a project—it's a state of being.

An abstract concept exploring identity loss, existential silence, and undefined transformation.  
There is no clear origin, no linear story—only presence.

---

## 🕳️ Concept

Wazy represents:

- **Undefined Change** — A shift without cause or explanation  
- **Memory Void** — No past, no trace, no history  
- **Existential Stillness** — A frozen state of awareness  
- **Non-returnability** — What’s lost cannot be restored  

---

## ⚙️ Philosophy

> Not everything needs a function.  
> Not everything needs a purpose.  

Wazy exists in the space between logic and void.
